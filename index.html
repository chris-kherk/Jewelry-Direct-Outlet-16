Moissanite is rapidly becoming a favorite among jewelry enthusiasts—and for good reason. This gemstone is renowned for its unmatched sparkle, rivaling even diamonds in brilliance. Caring for Your Moissanite Jewelry

Moissanite is a durable gemstone, but it’s still important to care for it properly to maintain its brilliance. Here are a few tips:

• Cleaning: Use a soft brush and warm, soapy water to clean your moissanite jewelry. Avoid harsh chemicals or ultrasonic cleaners, which can damage the setting.

• Storage: Store your moissanite pieces separately in a soft pouch or jewelry box to prevent scratches.

• Wear: Moissanite is tough, but to keep your pieces looking their best, avoid wearing them during rough activities or when handling harsh chemicals.
